Rules

How to avoid getting banned

Rule #1

You must follow the Discord TOS at all times.

Rule #2

Please do not create drama in the chat. If an argument gets heated, take it to DMs.

Rule #3

Don't post NSFW content, copypastas, malicious files or anything illegal.

Rule #4

Please do not harrass other members in any way. If they ask you to stop, you stop. You must always listen to a member of staff also.

Rule #5

Please do not post TeamViewer IDs/Passwords, join.me links, game links, etc unless you have permission from an administrator or above.

Rule #6

When using the scammer.info website, please refrain from posting any fake/joke numbers or numbers which are not scams.

Rule #7

Your username/nickname must contain mostly English characters and be easilly mentionable in chat. Please also keep your nickname similar to your username.

Rule #8

Do not tag @Staff unless it's a serious issue that requires our immediate attention.

Rule #9

Try not to bring drama from other servers here, it just causes arguments. We do not support raiding, so do not discuss it within Aurora.

Rule #10

Use common sense; if something isn't listed on here but you feel it should be, don't do it before asking an administrator.

Rule #11

Do not use bot commands in a spammy manner, use #bot_commands for most things.
A couple funny/relevent commands is not considered spammy.

Rule #12

Please use a suitable profile picture, which does not blend into the background of Discord. This means no fully transparent images.

Rule #13

Do not use self-bot commands in Aurora.

Rule #14

Do not abuse your trusted features, or you may lose the role.

Rule #15

Aurora is not your personal army, nor is it the place to discuss your enemies; please keep this drama out of Aurora.
